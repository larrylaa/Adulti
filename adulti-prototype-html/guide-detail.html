IRA Contribution Rhythm

Keep contributions steady and aligned with your long-term plan.

Why this matters

Regular contributions matter more than timing the market. Consistency builds progress and reduces decision stress.

How to do it

  1. Set a monthly IRA amount that fits your budget.
  2. Schedule it right after payday.
  3. Review contribution limits once per year.

Watch out for

  • Missing contribution limits and income thresholds.
  • Pausing contributions too often after market swings.
